Hear me out!

Genre: Social, Card Game

Description:

Hear Me Out is a fun and engaging social game designed for friends to explore each other's tastes and preferences by guessing which inanimate objects they would "ship" together. Players take turns as the "shipper," to ship 2 cards out of the 3 chosen from a card deck while other players would guess the "ship", creating opportunities for surprises, laughter and connection among players.

Description:

Don't we all have moments where you just want to bounce on clouds each playing a different music? No score, no competition, no threat. Just you and your friend, bouncing on clouds, listening to music and enjoying time!

Serenity Sown!

Genre: Life simulation, gardening, and narrative exploration.

Description:

"In Serenity Sown" is a narrative-driven, meditative life simulation game that invites players into the tranquil setting of a rural village. It features single-player gameplay that blends gardening mechanics, relationship-building, and a poignant healing narrative. The game begins in a monochromatic world, reflecting the protagonist's mental state. Through small acts of care and connection, players restore vibrancy and vitality to the environment, paralleling the protagonist's emotional recovery.
