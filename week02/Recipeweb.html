Easy Homemade Gulab Jamun

Nothing beats the rich, syrupy delight of homemade Gulab Jamun. This classic dessert from the subcontinent is a go-to treat for Eid, special occasions, or whenever youâ€™re craving something sweet.

Ingredients Needed

  • Milk Powder
  • All Purpose Flour
  • Baking Powder
  • Egg
  • Ghee or Clarified Butter
  • Water
  • Sugar
  • Cardamom Pods
  • Oil for Frying

How to make it?

  1. Make the dough
  2. Form balls
  3. Fry the Gulab Jamun
  4. Make the sugar syrup
  5. Soak the Gulab Jamun overnight
  6. Serve & Enjoy!
